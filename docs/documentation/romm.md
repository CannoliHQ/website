## What is RomM?

[RomM](https://romm.app) is a self-hosted ROM manager that lets you curate your game collection, manage saves, and
access everything through a web-based interface.

---

## Why should I care?

Here is a ~~personal opinion~~ **fact**, having too many games on your doo-dad is **dumb**.

There is no benefit to having 100s of games on your device all at once.

Browsing that many games sucks.

Organizing that many games sucks.

Curating art and metadata for that many games sucks.

_And the worst part_, if you happen to scrounge up the motivation to actually play something you end up with analysis paralysis
from too much choice.

RomM handles all of this for you off device. A clean, web-based interface for your entire collection. Metadata, art, organization, all handled.

---

## What will this integration do? { data-toc-label="What will this do?" }


It will let you keep your device free from the dangers of having too many games. 

It will also sync your save files!

Want a game that your device doesn't have? Pull it down wirelessly.

Playing a game across devices? Save Sync makes sure your progress follows you.
